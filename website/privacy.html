Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary

Dictto processes all audio locally on your Mac. We do not collect, store, or transmit your voice data or transcriptions. Your privacy is protected by design.

Data We Collect

Data Type Collected? Notes
Voice recordings No Processed locally, never transmitted
Transcribed text No Stays on your device
Usage analytics No We don't track how you use the app
Email (purchase) Yes* Only if you make a purchase, via Paddle

* Paddle collects payment info as our Merchant of Record. We only receive your email for license delivery.

How Dictto Works

All speech recognition happens entirely on your Mac using WhisperKit and Apple's CoreML framework. Your audio is:

  • Processed locally using on-device AI models
  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Deleted immediately after transcription

Third-Party Services

Paddle - Handles payment processing as our Merchant of Record. When you make a purchase, Paddle collects payment information according to their Privacy Policy.

WhisperKit - The speech recognition framework runs entirely on your device. No data is sent to OpenAI or any other service.

Data Storage

Dictto stores only:

  • Your preferences (model selection, language, hotkey) - locally in macOS preferences
  • Downloaded AI models - locally in ~/Library/Application Support/Dictto/
  • License information - locally on your device

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to delete. If you made a purchase and want your email removed from our records, contact privacy@dictto.app.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know - You can request what personal information we collect (answer: only email via Paddle if you purchase)
  • Right to Delete - You can request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out - We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Right to Non-Discrimination - We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@dictto.app. We will respond within 45 days.

Do Not Sell My Personal Information: We do not sell personal information. Dictto does not share your data with third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
