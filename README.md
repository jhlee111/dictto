# Dictto

**Voice to text, locally.** A macOS menu bar app for voice dictation powered by on-device AI.

No cloud. Your voice stays on your Mac.

## Features

### Always Ready in Your Menu Bar
Lives in your menu bar, ready whenever you need it. No dock icon clutter.

### 100% Local & Private
All transcription happens on your Mac using Apple's Neural Engine. Your voice never leaves your device.

### Global Hotkey
Press `Shift+Option+D` anywhere to start recording. Press again to transcribe. That's it.

### Multi-Language Support
Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese, and 90+ languages supported.

### User Dictionary
Add custom vocabulary for better recognition of names, technical terms, and domain-specific jargon.

### Smart Text Insertion
Transcribed text is automatically pasted into your active text field, or copied to clipboard as fallback.

|-------|------|----------|-------|----------|
| Tiny | 74 MB | Good | Fastest | Quick notes |
| Base | 141 MB | Better | Fast | General use |

## Requirements

- macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
- Apple Silicon Mac (M1/M2/M3/M4)

## Tips

- **Korean users**: Select "한국어" in Settings for better accuracy with short phrases
- **Specialized terms**: Create a User Dictionary with names and acronyms you use frequently

## Privacy

Dictto processes everything locally. No audio or text is sent to external servers. Ever.
